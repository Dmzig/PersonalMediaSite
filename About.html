What is this all about?


This site is a prototype I have been working on for enjoying my personal multi-media content. Although it currently only has a skeleton movies section, I hope to include Music, TV Shows, and other capabilities in the future to enhance the site. I would like to make a note that this site is not aimed at distributing content. I thought of this idea as more of a convenience application of being able to pull up my own collection of media no matter where I am or what device I am on. This site would eliminate the need to bring an external hard drive everywhere I want to enjoy a movie or some music. This site also would give me the ability to watch on a tablet, smartphone, or any other device that does not have USB connectivity for an external hard drive to enjoy my media.
